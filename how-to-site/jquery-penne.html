Best 30-minute
Penne alla Vodka Recipe

For 6 people

In the mood for a good Italian meal?

Concerned about getting takeout?

Learn this quick and delicious Penne alla Vodka recipe!

Before you know it, it'll be melting in your mouth!

Gather Your Ingredients

  • 16 oz (1 lb) of penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp. of butter
  • 2 tsp. of garlic
  • 1/2 tsp. of red pepper flakes
  • 28 oz of can plum tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of vodka
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of parmessian cheese
    (plus desired amount for serving)
  • 2 tbsp. of chooped parsley

Cook the Pasta

  • Grab a large pot and fill with water
  • Make water slightly salted
  • Look at pasta packaging for recommended boiling times
  • Make sure it's al dente (to the tooth)!

Prepare the sauce

  1. Melt butter on seperate pan that's on medium heat
  2. Add garlic and pepper flakes to the pan, cook until caramelized (gold)
  3. Make sure canned tomatoes are a smooth puree, add to the pan
  4. Add vodka to the pan and turn down to medium-low heat
  5. Add heavy cream and parmessian cheese while stirring
  6. keep sauce below boiling point until it thickens
  7. Make sure to keep an eye on the pasta while making the sauce!
  8. Taste the sauce with a hint of salt and pepper

Presentation

  • Pour vodka sauce over "al dente" pasta
  • Make sure to mix it in so it's evenly distributed
  • Sprinkle some grated parmessian cheese, red pepper, and chopped parsely on top

Serve or go buffet style!
